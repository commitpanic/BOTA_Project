ADIF Format Requirements
Required ADIF Fields:
  • MY_SIG: Must be "WWBOTA"
  • MY_SIG_INFO: Bunker reference (e.g., B/SP-0039)
  • OPERATOR or STATION_CALLSIGN: Your callsign
  • CALL: Hunter's callsign
  • QSO_DATE: Date of contact
  • TIME_ON: Time of contact (UTC)
Optional Fields:
  • MODE: Radio mode (SSB, CW, FM, etc.)
  • BAND: Radio band (40m, 20m, etc.)
Example ADIF Record:
<CALL:6>SP3BLZ <QSO_DATE:8>20250104 <TIME_ON:4>1922 <MODE:3>SSB <BAND:3>40m <MY_SIG:6>WWBOTA <MY_SIG_INFO:9>B/SP-0039 <OPERATOR:6>SP3FCK <EOR>
